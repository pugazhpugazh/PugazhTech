Anand Homes — Real Estate Website

Project Information

  • Category: Web Development
  • Client: Anand Homes
  • Industry: Real Estate
  • Location: Chennai, Tamil Nadu
  • Project Date: 2025
  • Project URL: anandhomes.co.in

Technologies Used

  • WordPress CMS
  • Custom Theme Development
  • Responsive CSS3 / HTML5
  • Contact Form with CRM Integration
  • SEO Optimization

About the Client

Anand Homes is a trusted real estate developer based in Chennai with over 12+ years of experience in the industry. Founded by a professional with prior experience at leading brands like Casagrand, Pooja Foundations, and Vijay Raja Homes, the company focuses on ethical practices, transparent dealings, and delivering quality homes in strategic locations like Kelambakkam and Pudupakkam.

Project Overview

PugazhTech was tasked with building a modern, professional website that would establish Anand Homes' digital presence and generate quality leads for their residential projects — "Six Meadows" in Kelambakkam and "Thendral Avenue" on Pudupakkam-Vandalur Road. The site needed to convey trust, showcase projects visually, and make it easy for potential buyers to enquire.

Key Features Delivered:

  • Fully responsive design optimized for mobile home buyers
  • Individual project pages with floor plans, pricing & gallery
  • Lead capture enquiry form with instant notifications
  • Testimonials section showcasing real customer reviews
  • Founder's profile & company story to build buyer trust
  • Blog section for real estate insights & SEO traffic
  • FAQ section addressing common buyer concerns
  • Google Maps integration for project locations

Results:

  • 60% increase in online enquiries within the first month
  • Improved brand credibility with professional online presence
  • Reduced dependency on third-party property portals

Uthavum Ullangal Trust — Non-Profit Website

Project Information

  • Category: Web Development
  • Client: Uthavum Ullangal Charitable Trust
  • Industry: Non-Profit / Charity
  • Location: Tamil Nadu, India
  • Project Date: 2024
  • Project URL: uthavumullangaltrust.org

Technologies Used

  • WordPress with Custom Theme
  • WooCommerce for Donations
  • Responsive Mobile-First Design
  • WhatsApp API Integration
  • Gallery & Event Management Plugins

About the Client

Uthavum Ullangal Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, food distribution, and clean water initiatives. With an active network of 215+ local "Clean Heart" branches, the trust runs campaigns for poor children's education, charity for better lives, and support for differently-abled individuals.

Project Overview

The trust needed a professional website to expand their reach, attract donors and volunteers, and showcase their campaigns and impact stories. PugazhTech designed a warm, trust-inspiring website that communicates their mission clearly and makes it easy for visitors to donate or join as volunteers.

Key Features Delivered:

  • Donation system with bank account details & WhatsApp link for monthly membership
  • Campaign showcase pages with impact stories and galleries
  • Volunteer registration and "Become a Volunteer" form
  • Event schedule section for upcoming trust activities
  • Portfolio of initiatives: education, food, healthcare, eco-system support
  • Blog & news section for updates and awareness articles
  • Mobile-responsive design for rural area accessibility
  • WhatsApp integration for instant donor communication

Results:

  • 3x increase in volunteer sign-ups after website launch
  • Streamlined donation process with direct bank transfer info
  • Social media traffic improved by 150% through campaign pages

VG Logistics — Global Freight & Export Website

Project Information

  • Category: Web Development
  • Client: VG Logistics
  • Industry: Logistics & Transportation
  • Location: Thirumazisai, Chennai
  • Project Date: 2026
  • Project URL: vglogistics.netlify.app

Technologies Used

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Modern Responsive Framework
  • Netlify Deployment (JAMstack)
  • Supabase Backend for Quotes
  • Animated UI with CSS Transitions

About the Client

VG Logistics is a global freight and export solutions company operating 24/7, specializing in ocean freight, air freight, customs clearance, import/export services, and warehousing. They serve businesses that need reliable international shipping and seamless logistics management across worldwide trade routes.

Project Overview

VG Logistics required a bold, professional website that reflects their global reach and reliability. PugazhTech crafted a modern, dark-themed website with striking red accents, dropdown navigation for services and solutions, and an integrated quote request system backed by a Supabase database to capture business leads.

Key Features Delivered:

  • Professional dark theme with bold typography and red accent branding
  • Multi-page structure: Home, Services, Solutions, About, Contact
  • Dropdown navigation for Ocean Freight, Air Freight, Warehousing
  • Instant Quote request form connected to Supabase database
  • Service detail pages for Customs Clearance, Import/Export
  • Global Network showcase for worldwide coverage
  • Shipment tracking call-to-action section
  • Fully responsive design optimized for business clients

Results:

  • Professional brand presence established for global trade clients
  • Quote requests captured directly to database — 40+ leads in first week
  • Mobile-friendly experience serving business clients on the go
